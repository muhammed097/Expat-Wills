Privacy Policy

Your data protection and privacy rights in the UAE

Effective Date: January 27, 2026

At ExpatWills, we are committed to protecting your privacy in accordance with UAE Federal Decree-Law No. 45 of 2021 on the Protection of Personal Data (PDPL). This policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information.

1. Information We Collect

We collect personal information that you provide to us when registering for our services, including but not limited to:

  • Full Name and Contact Information
  • Emirate of Residence
  • Email Address and Phone Number
  • Specific details regarding your UAE-based assets for consultation purposes

2. How We Use Your Data

The information collected is used solely to:

  • Facilitate Will registration and legal consultations.
  • Communicate with you regarding your application.
  • Provide updates on UAE inheritance laws that may affect you.
  • Coordinate with our licensed legal partners to draft your documents.

3. Data Sharing and UAE Law

We do not sell your personal data. We only share information with licensed UAE legal professionals and relevant government authorities (such as DIFC or ADJD) as required to finalize your Will registration.

4. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your data from unauthorized access, alteration, or disclosure, in compliance with UAE cybersecurity standards.

5. Your Rights

Under UAE law, you have the right to access, rectify, or request the deletion of your personal data held by us. To exercise these rights, please contact us at info@expatwills.com.

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy periodically. Significant changes will be communicated via our website.
